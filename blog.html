IBITIcoin early sale announcement (archived)

Planned start date: 01.07.2025

The original concept for IBITIcoin included an early token sale starting at a price of 0.10 USDT per token with additional bonuses for early supporters. As the project evolved, we moved away from fixed “phased” sales toward a direct public launch via a liquidity pool on PancakeSwap.

NFT jackpot airdrop concept

Initial concept date: 08.07.2025

The IBITI ecosystem includes the idea of an NFT jackpot, where active participants and early supporters can receive exclusive NFTs with additional benefits inside the ecosystem. The mechanics are being adapted to the current IBITI/USDT liquidity model and will be rolled out in separate campaigns.

From admin-controlled pricing to AMM

Transition point: 21.07.2025

Earlier drafts of the tokenomics included a temporary “fallback” price mechanism managed via an administrative panel. This approach has been discontinued in favor of a transparent automated market maker (AMM) model through a public liquidity pool. Today, IBITI price formation is handled entirely by the IBITI/USDT pool on BNB Smart Chain.

Integration with a leading DeFi platform

Launch date: 10.12.2025

As of 10 December 2025, IBITIcoin is available in one of the largest DeFi ecosystems on BNB Smart Chain: trading and liquidity are live in the IBITI/USDT pair on PancakeSwap (v2). This marks the transition from internal sale concepts to fully public, on-chain liquidity that anyone can access.
