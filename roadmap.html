Roadmap

Launch details

Q3 2025 – Early tokenomics & internal sale concept (archived)

Initial drafts of phase-based sales and fallback pricing were designed and tested internally. The concept was later replaced by a cleaner, fully on-chain liquidity model via PancakeSwap.

10.12.2025 – Public launch on PancakeSwap (completed)

IBITIcoin is officially traded on BNB Smart Chain in the IBITI / USDT pair on PancakeSwap (v2). The first public liquidity pool is live and serves as the primary entry point for new users.

Token contract: 0x47F2FFCb164b2EeCCfb7eC436Dfb3637a457B9bb

Open IBITI / USDT on PancakeSwap

2026 – Staking & on-chain rewards (in development)

Launch of on-chain staking modules for IBITI holders, with flexible reward mechanics and transparent contracts. Goal: provide long-term holders with sustainable, code-driven rewards without hidden emissions.

2026 – NFT utilities & discount mechanics (in development)

Activation of the NFT jackpot concept and utility NFTs: access perks, fee discounts, and bonus multipliers for active community members. NFTs are designed with limited lifetimes or activations and are burned after use.

2026 – DeFi integrations & ecosystem expansion (planned)

Integration of IBITIcoin into broader DeFi infrastructure on BNB Smart Chain and beyond: liquidity incentives, lending/borrowing options, partner protocols and analytics tools. Focus: real usage, not just listings for the sake of listings.

2026 – User interfaces & mobile access (planned)

Improving user experience with cleaner web and mobile-friendly interfaces for: trading, staking, NFT utilities and DAO participation. The goal is to make IBITI accessible not only to crypto-native users but also to people entering DeFi for the first time.
