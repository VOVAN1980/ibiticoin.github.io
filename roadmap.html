Launch of staking modules for IBITI holders with transparent reward mechanics. Focus: sustainability, clarity, and predictable rules — without hidden emissions.

Utility NFTs for access perks, fee discounts, and bonus multipliers for active community members. Some NFTs may be activation-based (consumed/burned after use) depending on the campaign design.

Status: In development

2026 – DeFi integrations & ecosystem expansion (planned)

Integration into broader DeFi infrastructure on BNB Smart Chain and beyond: analytics, partner protocols, liquidity tools, and optional integrations where they make real sense.

Status: Planned

2026 – UX upgrades & mobile-first access (planned)

Continued improvements to web UI, mobile DApp-browser experience, and simplified onboarding for users entering DeFi for the first time.
