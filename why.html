What are we building?

IBITIcoin is designed as more than just a token. The ecosystem roadmap includes utility modules built gradually and transparently. We prefer shipping working components over announcing empty promises.

  • Staking mechanisms to reward long-term holders
  • DAO governance for community participation in decisions
  • NFT-based utilities and access features
  • Additional on-chain tools as the ecosystem grows

Transparency & security

Transparency is a core principle of IBITIcoin: verified contracts, visible liquidity, public communication, and official links listed openly.

Security reminder: The IBITIcoin team never contacts users first via private messages. If someone messages you “from the team” first — treat it as a scam attempt. Always verify links only from official pages.
