IBITIcoin Whitepaper (Light Version)

IBITIcoin (IBITI) is a modular DeFi token on BNB Smart Chain (BEP-20, 8 decimals) with on-chain treasury, dynamic fees, staking, NFT utilities and DAO governance. This page is a readable summary of the full PDF whitepaper.

Network: BNB Smart Chain (BEP-20)
Token: IBITI (8 decimals)
Contract: 0x47F2FFCb164b2EeCCfb7eC436Dfb3637a457B9bb

1. Vision & Problem

Most small-cap tokens either have zero real mechanics or hide all logic off-chain. IBITIcoin goes the opposite way: everything that touches liquidity, fees, rewards and governance is implemented in auditable smart contracts.

IBITI is designed as the core asset of a broader ecosystem: staking, NFT discounts, DAO and cross-chain bridges are all implemented as separate contracts that can evolve over time.

2.1 Treasury-centric design

A large share of the supply is held by the on-chain treasury contract. From there, tokens are gradually streamed to:

  • Liquidity & reserve wallets
  • Staking rewards and ecosystem incentives
  • DAO/Community programs
  • Team & contributor vesting contracts

All movements from the treasury are done through explicit transactions (e.g. treasurySend) signed from the owner’s hardware wallet.

3. Utility

Staking NFT discounts DAO voting Buyback & burn

3.1 Staking module

Holders can stake IBITI in the on-chain staking module to earn additional IBITI rewards and qualify for lower trading fees.

3.2 NFT utilities

The IBITINFT and NFTDiscount contracts allow IBITI-denominated NFT purchases and NFT-based fee discounts (e.g. loyal users, early supporters).

3.3 Buyback & burn

The BuybackManager can periodically swap stablecoins for IBITI on PancakeSwap and burn a configurable percentage of the purchased tokens, reducing effective circulating supply over time.

4. Roadmap (high-level)

  • Phase 1 – Launch: token deployment, initial liquidity, basic website, explorer data.
  • Phase 2 – Utilities: public launch of staking and NFT modules; first on-chain DAO proposals.
  • Phase 3 – Ecosystem: integrations, partner campaigns, listings and cross-chain experiments.
  • Phase 4 – Scaling: community-driven governance, recurring buyback/burn and long-term liquidity program.

5. Fee Model

Trading fees are handled by a dedicated FeeManager contract. It supports:

  • Separate base fees for buys and sells
  • Discounts for staking, VIP users and long-term holders
  • Extra fee for large “whale” sells
  • Dynamic coefficient based on recent volume/volatility
  • Optional NFT-based discounts

Final fee is always clamped between 0% and 50% and fully transparent on-chain.

6. Security & Risk

  • Contracts use OpenZeppelin security-audited primitives.
  • Critical modules are pausable for emergency response.
  • No custodial user deposits: users always keep control of wallets.
  • Contract source is verified on BscScan.

IBITIcoin is an experimental DeFi project. Smart-contract risk, market risk and liquidity risk always exist. Never invest funds you cannot afford to lose.
