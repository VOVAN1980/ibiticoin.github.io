How to Buy IBITIcoin (IBITI)

Simple step-by-step guide to buying IBITI on BNB Smart Chain using PancakeSwap. No presale, no airdrops – only on-chain trading via the official IBITI / USDT pool.

Quick steps

  1. Install a Web3 wallet (MetaMask / Trust Wallet / Binance Web3 Wallet).
  2. Switch your wallet to BNB Smart Chain / BSC.
  3. Top up your wallet with USDT (BEP-20) and a small amount of BNB for gas.
  4. Open PancakeSwap and connect your wallet.
  5. Import the official IBITI token using the contract address.
  6. Swap USDT → IBITI and confirm the transaction.
  7. Add IBITI to your wallet view (if it is not shown automatically).

1. Prepare a Web3 wallet

Use a non-custodial wallet that supports BNB Smart Chain:

  • MetaMask (browser & mobile)
  • Trust Wallet
  • Binance Web3 Wallet or any WalletConnect-compatible wallet

Create a new wallet or import an existing one and store your seed phrase securely. Nobody from the IBITI team will ever ask for your seed phrase or private keys.

2. Switch to BNB Smart Chain

In your wallet, select the BNB Smart Chain / BSC network. If you use MetaMask and do not have BSC in the list, you can add it via the official BNB Chain documentation or helper tools like Chainlist.

3. Fund your wallet (USDT + BNB)

To buy IBITI you need:

  • USDT (BEP-20) – the token you will swap into IBITI;
  • BNB – a small amount to pay gas fees on BSC.

You can withdraw USDT and BNB from centralized exchanges (for example, Binance) directly to your wallet address on the BNB Smart Chain network.

4. Open PancakeSwap and connect wallet

Go to the official PancakeSwap website: pancakeswap.finance. In the top menu open Trade → Swap and click Connect Wallet. Choose your wallet (MetaMask, Trust Wallet, WalletConnect, etc.) and confirm the connection.

5. Import the IBITI token

In the swap interface:

  • In the bottom token field click on the token selector.
  • Paste the IBITI contract address:
    0x47F2FFCb164b2EeCCfb7eC436Dfb3637a457B9bb
  • Confirm the import. Always double-check the address.
Official IBITI token details:
Network: BNB Smart Chain (BEP-20)
Name: IBITIcoin
Symbol: IBITI
Decimals: 8
Contract: 0x47F2FFCb164b2EeCCfb7eC436Dfb3637a457B9bb

6. Swap USDT to IBITI

Now you can swap:

  • Top token: USDT
  • Bottom token: IBITI

Enter the amount of USDT you want to spend. For the first trade it is reasonable to test a small amount and then increase the size once you are comfortable with the process.

If PancakeSwap shows price impact warnings, reduce the trade size or wait for more liquidity.

7. Confirm the transaction

Click Swap in PancakeSwap and confirm the transaction in your wallet (MetaMask, Trust Wallet, etc.). Once the transaction is confirmed on BSC, you will receive IBITI in your wallet.

8. Add IBITI to your wallet view

If you do not see IBITI in the token list, add it manually:

  • Token contract: 0x47F2FFCb164b2EeCCfb7eC436Dfb3637a457B9bb
  • Name: IBITIcoin
  • Symbol: IBITI
  • Decimals: 8
Always verify you are on the official PancakeSwap website and that the IBITI contract address exactly matches the one above. There may be fake tokens with similar names.
