How to Buy IBITIcoin (IBITI)

Step-by-step guide to buying IBITI on BNB Smart Chain using PancakeSwap.

Network: BNB Smart Chain • DEX: PancakeSwap v2 • Pair: IBITI / USDT

Quick Steps

1Prepare a Web3 wallet

Install a non-custodial wallet that supports BNB Smart Chain (BSC):

  • MetaMask (browser & mobile)
  • Trust Wallet
  • Binance Web3 Wallet or any WalletConnect compatible wallet

Create a new wallet or import your existing one and save your seed phrase securely. Nobody from IBITI will ever ask for your seed phrase.

2Switch to BNB Smart Chain

In your wallet, select the BNB Smart Chain / BSC network. If you use MetaMask and don’t have BSC yet, you can add it via “Add network” or through a helper like Chainlist.

3Fund your wallet (USDT + BNB for gas)

To buy IBITI you need:

  • USDT (BEP-20) – the token you swap into IBITI.
  • BNB – a small amount to pay gas fees on BSC.

You can withdraw USDT and BNB from a centralized exchange (Binance, etc.) to your wallet address using the BNB Smart Chain / BEP-20 network.

4Open PancakeSwap and connect wallet

Go to the official PancakeSwap website: pancakeswap.finance and click Connect Wallet. Choose your wallet (MetaMask, WalletConnect, etc.).

5Import the IBITI token

In the swap interface:

  • In the bottom token field click on the token selector.
  • Paste the IBITI contract address:
    0x47F2FFCb164b2EeCCfb7eC436Dfb3637a457B9bb
  • Confirm the import. Always double-check the address.
Official IBITI token details:
Network: BNB Smart Chain (BEP-20)
Name: IBITIcoin
Symbol: IBITI
Decimals: 8
Contract: 0x47F2FFCb164b2EeCCfb7eC436Dfb3637a457B9bb

6Swap USDT to IBITI

Now you can swap:

  • Top token: USDT
  • Bottom token: IBITI

Enter the amount of USDT you want to spend. Start small for your first trade (for example 1–5 USDT) to test everything.

If PancakeSwap shows price impact warnings, reduce the trade size or wait for more liquidity.

7Confirm the transaction in your wallet

Click Swap, then confirm in your wallet (MetaMask, Trust Wallet, etc.). Once the transaction is confirmed on BSC, you will receive IBITI in your wallet.

8Add IBITI to your wallet view

If you don’t see IBITI in the token list, add it manually:

  • Token contract: 0x47F2FFCb164b2EeCCfb7eC436Dfb3637a457B9bb
  • Name: IBITIcoin
  • Symbol: IBITI
  • Decimals: 8
Important:
Always verify you are on the official PancakeSwap website and that the IBITI contract address exactly matches the one above. There may be fake tokens with a similar name.
