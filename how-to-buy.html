What is Website Promo?
You buy IBITI directly on the official IBITIcoin website using an on-chain promo router (UUPS proxy). It can provide a bonus and referral reward while keeping the USDT cashflow transparent on-chain.
Promo is temporary: the promo can be disabled on-chain (promoActive=false or promoEndTime reached). If promo is inactive, you can always buy IBITI via PancakeSwap (switch the tab above).

1Prepare a Web3 wallet

Install a non-custodial wallet that supports BNB Smart Chain (BSC):

  • MetaMask (browser & mobile)
  • Trust Wallet
  • Binance Web3 Wallet or any WalletConnect compatible wallet

Save your seed phrase securely. Nobody from IBITI will ever ask for your seed phrase.

2Switch to BNB Smart Chain

In your wallet, select the BNB Smart Chain / BSC network.

3Fund your wallet (USDT + BNB for gas)

To buy via promo you need:

  • USDT (BEP-20) — payment token.
  • BNB — a small amount for gas fees.

4Open the official website and connect wallet

Go to the official IBITIcoin website and open the promo buy flow. Connect your wallet and choose Promo Buy. (Always double-check the domain and links.)

5Enter USDT amount (and optional referrer)

Enter the amount of USDT you want to spend. Optionally paste a referrer address/link if you have one. The router uses the IBITI/USDT pair as a market reference and applies promo rules on-chain.

6Confirm in MetaMask (usually 2 confirmations)

  • 1) USDT Approve — one-time (or when allowance is insufficient). This grants the router permission to spend your USDT.
  • 2) Purchase — the actual buy transaction.

Note: Approve and Purchase are separate confirmations. Bonus/referral payouts are executed as token transfers during the purchase transaction (same tx hash).

7Receipt (purchase check)

After the purchase is confirmed, the website shows an IBITI Receipt with your transaction hash and a summary (paid USDT, received IBITI incl. bonus, effective price) plus direct verification links.

Where does my USDT go?
In the purchase transaction your USDT is forwarded to the Reserve Wallet. Liquidity additions (if performed) are done by Reserve Wallet as a separate on-chain transaction.
